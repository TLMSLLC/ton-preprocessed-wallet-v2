## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in this project, please
report it to us privately so we can investigate and fix the issue before any
public disclosure.

### How to report

- Email: [security@example.com](mailto:security@example.com)
- Or use GitHub’s “Report a vulnerability” feature, if available for this
  repository.

When reporting, please include:

- A description of the vulnerability and the affected component(s) or version(s)
- Steps to reproduce the issue, including any relevant configuration
- The potential impact of the vulnerability
- Any available proof-of-concept code or screenshots
- Your preferred contact information

### What to expect

- **Acknowledgment:** We will acknowledge receipt of your report within
  3 business days.
- **Assessment:** We will investigate and assess the impact, typically within
  10 business days.
- **Status updates:** We will provide updates at least every 7 days while the
  investigation and remediation are in progress.
- **Disclosure:** Once a fix is available and deployed, we will coordinate a
  disclosure timeline with you. Please do not publicly disclose details of the
  vulnerability until we have confirmed that a fix has been released or agreed
  on a disclosure date together.
